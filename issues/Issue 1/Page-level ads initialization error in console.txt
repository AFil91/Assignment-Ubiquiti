Bug Title
----------

Preconditions
--------------
Chrome 146.0.7680.178

Steps to reproduce
-------------------
1. Open https://www.inyourpocket.com/sweden/stockholm
2. Open DevTools → Console

Result
-------
Error: "TagError: adsbygoogle.push() error: Only one 'enable_page_level_ads' allowed per page."

Expected
---------
No error. Ads initialize once per page load.

Notes
