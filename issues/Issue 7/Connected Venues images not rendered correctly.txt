Bug Title
----------
Connected Venues images broken on smaller window sizes

Preconditions
--------------
Chrome 146.0.7680.178
Browser window resized below 1376x960
User navigated to Articles via "View all articles"

Steps to reproduce
-------------------
1. Open any article
2. Scroll to "Connected Venues"

Result
-------

Expected
