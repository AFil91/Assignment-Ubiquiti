Bug Title
----------
Removing a category filter causes page to scroll to the top

Preconditions
--------------
Chrome 146.0.7680.178
User is on a page with the Category filter (See more, Eat, or Nightlife)
At least one category is selected

Steps to reproduce
-------------------
1. Tap the X on any selected category tag

Result
-------
Page jumps to the top.

Expected
---------
Page stays in position; no scroll jump.
