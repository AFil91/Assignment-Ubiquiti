Bug Title
----------
"You might also be interested in" section overflows to the right on large windows

Preconditions
--------------
Chrome 146.0.7680.178
Browser window resized below 1376x960
User navigated to Articles via "View all articles"

Steps to reproduce
-------------------
1. Scroll to "You might also be interested in"
2. Click "View all"

Result
-------
Items overflow horizontally to the right even at window sizes large enough to show them normally.

Expected
---------
Items wrap or display below each other — horizontal overflow should only occur on small/mobile screens.
