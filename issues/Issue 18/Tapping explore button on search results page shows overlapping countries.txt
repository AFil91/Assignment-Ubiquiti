Bug Title
----------
Explore button on search results shows overlapping country names (mobile)

Preconditions
--------------
Mobile device
User has at least one recent search for a venue (e.g. Herman's Vegetariska Restaurang)

Steps to reproduce
-------------------
1. Open Search
2. Tap a recent search entry
3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Explore"

Result
-------
Country names overlap each other.

Expected
---------
Country names display without overlapping.
