Bug Title
----------
[mobile] Map button on articles with no map scrolls to page top

Preconditions
--------------
Mobile device (Samsung S23, Android 15)
User is on an article without a map (e.g. "Exploring Stockholm in spring")

Steps to reproduce
-------------------
1. Tap the map button

Result
-------
Page scrolls to the top.

Expected
---------
Map button should be hidden for articles that have no map.
