Bug Title
----------
Ads disappear after navigating to "See and do" and back

Preconditions
--------------
Chrome 146.0.7680.178
User is on the main page

Steps to reproduce
-------------------
1. Scroll to "See and do" section
2. Click "View all"
3. Press browser Back button

Result
-------
First 2 ads are missing from the main page.

Expected
---------
Ads remain visible after navigating back.
