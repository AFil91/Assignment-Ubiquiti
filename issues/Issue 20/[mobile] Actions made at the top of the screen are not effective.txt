Bug Title
----------
[mobile] Buttons at the top of the screen are unresponsive

Preconditions
--------------
Mobile device (Samsung S23, Android 15)
User is on the "See and do" page

Steps to reproduce
-------------------
1. Scroll until action buttons reach the top of the screen
2. Tap any button

Result
-------
Taps have no effect.

Expected
---------
Buttons respond normally regardless of scroll position.
