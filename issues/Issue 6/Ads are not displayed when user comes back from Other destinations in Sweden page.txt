Bug Title
----------
Ads disappear after returning from "Other destinations in Sweden" page

Preconditions
--------------
Chrome 146.0.7680.178
User is on the main page with ads visible

Steps to reproduce
-------------------
1. Scroll to "Check out other destinations in Sweden"
2. Click "See more"
3. Press browser Back button

Result
-------
Ads no longer appear on the main page.

Expected
---------
Ads remain visible after navigating back.
